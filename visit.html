truffle season

Our neighbours dig truffles every mid June to end of July of each year. Book a table at our restaurant and join us for lunch to enjoy truffles on our menu, mellow and good with a glass of aged Black Estate wines. Tickets to our Matariki Truffle Lunch on 24 June have sold out.


waipara valley vineyard trail

Visit the Waipara Valley Vineyard Trail on your bike or on foot. It starts at the Waipara Domain and winds its way through Waipara Springs, Greystone, Muddy Water, Mountford and to Black Estate. The trail loops across SH1 to Kings Road and back to Waipara Domain. (Cross SH1 at your own risk).
