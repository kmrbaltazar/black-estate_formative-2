come visit us!

We're making pure living wines, grown at our three small biodynamic vineyards. We farm with nature, tuning in and taking steps to give back to and regenerate the land we are simply guardians of. Our wish is for the land to be full of natural life during and after our time here. While farming and growing grapes, we can sequester carbon, contribute to a balancing of this planet and grop fruit that has quality and flavour. Nicholas and his team guide our wines naturally in the cellar so that we can present the fruit that was picked without too much interference. If the wines can speak honestly of their place and season, we can share the connection we have to these places with you! No additions or manipulations are made to our wines, nor fining or filtering, except in some cases, minimal sulphur at bottling. Home, Damsteep and Netherwood vineyards lie on the hills of Omihi, North Canterbury, New Zealand. Certified organic wine with Biogro 5202 and in convention with Demeter.
